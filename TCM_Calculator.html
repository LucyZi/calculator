Transitional Care Management (TCM) Calculator

Utilizing the HCCI Transitional Care Management (TCM) Calculator will provide healthcare practices with insights into potential financial sustainability and practice growth by optimizing reimbursement incentives, reducing hospital readmissions and emergency department visits, improving operational efficiency, and enhancing patient outcomes through timely and coordinated post-discharge care. This proactive management helps ensure smooth transitions, fosters long-term patient retention, and supports better patient care continuity.
