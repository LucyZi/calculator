Chronic Care Management (CCM) Calculator

Chronic Care Management (CCM) encompasses coordinated activities aimed at effectively managing the health of patients with chronic conditions. It facilitates comprehensive oversight and resource integration, especially for those needing sustained medical attention across diverse settings, including home-based medical care (HBMC). Crucial in transitioning from episodic to holistic care, CCM emphasizes continuous patient monitoring and preventive management.

Utilizing the HCCI Chronic Care Management (CCM) calculator will provide healthcare practices with insights into potential financial sustainability and practice growth by optimizing reimbursement incentives, reducing hospitalizations and emergency department visits, improving operational efficiency, and fostering long-term patient retention through proactive management.
